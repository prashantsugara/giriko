Privacy Policy: If Wishes Could Kill (Girigo)
Effective Date: May 12, 2026

Welcome to Girigo, the official interactive experience for the "If Wishes Could Kill" series. Your privacy and the sanctity of your rituals are important to us.

1. Information We Collect
To manifest your rituals in the void, we collect:

Account Information: Your name, email, and profile data provided during Google Sign-In.
User Generated Content (UGC): Videos recorded via the ritual camera and captions you provide.
Interaction Data: Likes, follows, and comments performed within the app.
Technical Data: Approximate location (via IP for country detection), device identifiers (for notifications and ads), and app performance logs.

2. How We Use Your Data
To provide the "If Wishes Could Kill" social ritual experience.
To detect your approximate location for localized ritual feeds.
To personalize the void using your chosen "Ritual Accents."
To show relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.

3. User Generated Content (UGC) Policy
Girigo is a platform for raw truth. However, to maintain the sanctuary:

Moderation: We reserve the right to remove content that is harmful, illegal, or violates our community standards.
Reporting: Users can flag any ritual for review using the "Report" button. Flagged content is reviewed by our moderators within 24 hours.
Blocking: Users can block any other soul to prevent further interaction.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties
We use trusted third-party services to sustain the void:

Supabase: For database management and authentication.
Cloudflare R2: For secure storage of your ritual manifestations.
Google AdMob: For providing rewarded and native advertisements.
5. Your Rights
You may request the deletion of your account and all associated rituals at any time via the "Altar" (Profile) settings or by contacting our support team.
